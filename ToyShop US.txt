ToyShop: an e-commerce to buy toys.


[User Stories]

> Search for a product
  As a customer
  I want search for a product
  So that I can add it to my basket
  
> Search by category
  As a customer
  I want search products by category
  So that I can find similar products
  
> Add to basket
  As a customer
  I want add a product to my basket
  So that I can buy it
  
> Remove from basket
  As a customer
  I want remove a product from my basket
  So that I don't need to buy it

> Checkout
  As a customer
  I want checkout my basket
  So that I can finish my purchase
  
> Sign in
  As a customer
  I want sign in
  So that I can finish my purchase

> Payment
  As a customer
  I want pay my purchase
  So that I can receive the bought items

> Notification
  As a customer
  I want receive a notification about my purchase
  So that I can register and follow the status
